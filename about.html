About Our Store

The Valorant Merch Store was created for fans who want to bring their in-game passion into real life. We offer stylish, high-quality Valorant-inspired apparel and gaming accessories designed for comfort, performance, and everyday wear.

This website was developed as an activity for the Platform Technologies course, showcasing the use of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript functions and events to build an interactive web application.

Our goal is to combine clean design with smooth user interaction while delivering a professional and enjoyable browsing experience for gamers.
